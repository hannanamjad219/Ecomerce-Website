PRIVACY POLICY

Transparent. Simple. Secure.

Who We Are and Our Commitment

At TechVerse, we’re tech enthusiasts just like you. We know how annoying it is to have your data handled poorly, so we’ve made a simple promise: we treat your information the way we’d want ours treated. This policy explains what we collect when you shop for your favorite gear and, more importantly, why we need it. Our goal is to be 100% transparent so you can focus on enjoying your new hardware.

The Information We Collect

When you browse our store or make a purchase, we collect the basics: your name, your email so we can send you your receipt, and your physical address so we know where to ship your package. If you create an account, we keep your order history handy so you can track your tech journey with us. We also collect some technical bits like your IP address to help keep our store secure and prevent fraud, nothing more, nothing less.

How We Use Your Data

We don’t sell your data to third parties. Period. We use your information strictly to get your orders processed, packed, and delivered. We might send you an occasional email if we have a massive sale or a new product launch we think you’ll love, but you can opt out of those with a single click at any time. Your data is used to make your shopping experience smoother, not to flood your inbox or track you across the web.

Keeping Your Tech Secure

Security is in our DNA. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal details during checkout. While no website is a 100% fortress, we work around the clock to ensure our "digital walls" are high and your data stays private. We partner with trusted payment processors so that your sensitive credit card information never even touches our servers, it goes straight to the bank where it belongs.

You’re in Control

It’s your data, and you have the final say. If you want to know exactly what info we have on you, just ask. If you want us to update your address or delete your account entirely, send us an email. We’ll handle it quickly and without any hassle. We believe that privacy isn’t just a legal requirement; it’s a fundamental part of being a modern, trustworthy brand.
